TASK 4 PART B

Social Media Responsibility Checklist

The Do’s (Best Practices)


1.Do use privacy settings to strictly control who can see your personal posts and contact information.


2.Do think before you post; ask yourself if you would be comfortable with a future employer or professor seeing the content.


3.Do engage in respectful and constructive discussions, even when you disagree with others online.


4.Do give proper credit to original creators when sharing or using their work, photos, or intellectual ideas.


5.Do use your platforms professionally to showcase your skills, academic projects, and certifications.

The Don’ts (Avoid These)


1.Don’t post offensive, discriminatory, or inflammatory content that could negatively impact your digital reputation.


2.Don’t share sensitive personal data like your home address, phone number, bank details, or Aadhaar number.


3.Don’t participate in or encourage cyberbullying, online harassment, or the spreading of unverified rumors.


4.Don’t vent about your university, professors, or workplace in a public or unprofessional manner.


5.Don’t post photos or videos of other people without getting their explicit permission and consent first.
